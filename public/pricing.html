Frequently asked questions

How do you get your data?

We monitor official NHS Find a Dentist pages on a schedule. When a practice appears to accept new NHS patients, we email you.

Will you send marketing emails?

No. We only send the alerts you requested, plus essential service messages. You can unsubscribe in one click.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are month-to-month via Stripe. You can cancel anytime from your receipt email or by contacting support.

Which areas do you cover?

We cover England where NHS dentist listings are publicly available. Add your postcode and radius; we’ll monitor nearby practices.
