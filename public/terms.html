Terms of Use

Last updated: December 2025

1. About this service

DentistRadar is a simple notification tool that helps you find NHS dentists by checking availability and sending alerts when new spaces appear. It does not book appointments for you or guarantee that a place will still be available when you contact a practice.

2. No medical or legal advice

DentistRadar does not provide medical, dental, financial, or legal advice. Any decisions about your care should be made directly with qualified professionals and official NHS channels.

3. Your responsibilities

  • Provide accurate contact details when signing up for alerts.
  • Use the service in a lawful and reasonable way.
  • Contact practices directly to confirm availability and register.

4. Service limitations

Availability data may change quickly and is dependent on external sources, such as NHS England listings or practice information. We cannot guarantee that:

  • Alerts will always be delivered or received by your email provider.
  • Spaces will still be available when you contact a practice.
  • The service will be free from interruption, bugs, or errors at all times.

5. Acceptable use

You agree not to:

  • Misuse the service for automated scraping or high-volume automated requests.
  • Attempt to reverse engineer or interfere with the operation of the service.
  • Use the service in a way that could harass or harm practices or other users.

6. Changes to the service

The features, coverage, and pricing of DentistRadar may change over time. Where changes are significant, we will aim to communicate them clearly via the website or by email where appropriate.

7. Contact

If you have questions about these terms, you can contact: support@dentistradar.co.uk.
