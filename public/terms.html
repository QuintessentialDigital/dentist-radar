Terms of Service

1. Service

Dentist Radar (“we”, “our”, “us”) monitors public sources and notifies users when NHS dental practices appear to be accepting new patients near their chosen postcode(s). We do not book appointments and cannot guarantee availability.

2. Use

You agree to use the service lawfully, not to interfere with its operation, and not to misuse data. You must be 16+ or have parental consent.

3. Alerts & Accuracy

We try to provide timely notifications, but information can change quickly. Always contact the practice to confirm before travelling.

4. Plans & Payments

Free plan supports one postcode. Paid plans (when enabled) grant additional features as described. Prices may change prospectively. Refunds are handled per UK consumer law.
