How DentistRadar works

Under the hood there’s a lot of automation. For you, it’s just a postcode, an email and a clear alert when something opens.

1

You enter your postcode

Tell us roughly where you are and how far you can realistically travel for a dentist.

2

We watch NHS information

We keep an eye on public NHS data and other signals instead of you refreshing pages and calling practices repeatedly.

3

You get an alert when there’s an opening

When a practice looks likely to be taking NHS patients, we email you with clear details.

4

You move quickly — or wait for the next

You choose whether to act on that alert. If it doesn’t work out, we keep watching for the next one.
