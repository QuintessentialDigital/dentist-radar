Privacy Policy

Effective: 27 October 2025

What we collect

  • Your email address (to send alerts).
  • Your postcode(s) and search radius (to look up nearby practices).
  • Basic usage logs to keep the service reliable and secure.

How we use data

  • To send the alerts you requested when availability changes.
  • To improve the service (aggregate, non-identifying stats).
  • To keep systems secure and prevent abuse.

Sharing

We don’t sell your data. We may use reputable processors (e.g., email delivery) to operate the service. They are contractually bound to protect your data.

Retention

We keep your alert details while your alert is active. You can request deletion via support@dentistradar.co.uk or unsubscribe from emails to stop alerts.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, deletion, or portability of your data under UK GDPR. Contact: support@dentistradar.co.uk.

Contact

For questions about this policy or your data, email support@dentistradar.co.uk.
