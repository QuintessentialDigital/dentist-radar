Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 October 2025

1. Who we are

Dentist Radar provides alert notifications about potential NHS dentist availability near your chosen postcode(s).

2. What we collect

  • Email address (to send alerts)
  • Postcode(s) and chosen radius
  • Technical logs (e.g., IP, timestamps) for security and troubleshooting

3. How we use your data

  • To deliver alerts you requested
  • To improve and secure the service
  • To communicate important service updates

4. Lawful basis

We process your data under UK GDPR on the basis of your consent and/or our legitimate interests in providing the service.

5. Storage & Providers

We use reputable providers to run our service (e.g., hosting, databases, email delivery). Where applicable, these providers process limited data as our processors.

  • Email delivery (e.g., Postmark or similar): sends alert emails on our behalf.
  • Database hosting (e.g., managed cloud database): stores alert configuration data (email, postcode, radius).

6. Retention

We keep your alert details while your alert is active. If you delete your alert, we remove it from our active system; minimal logs may remain for security and audit.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you may have rights to access, correct, delete or restrict processing of your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies only (e.g., for basic site functionality). We do not use cookies for advertising.

9. Contact

To contact us about privacy or to exercise your rights, email: privacy@dentistradar.co.uk

10. Changes

We may update this Policy; a new “Last updated” date will appear at the top of this page.

This page is provided for information only and is not legal advice.
