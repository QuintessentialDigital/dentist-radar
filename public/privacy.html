Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Who we are

DentistRadar is a tool that helps you discover NHS dentists accepting new patients and receive email alerts when availability changes.

2. What data we collect

When you use DentistRadar, we may collect:

  • Your email address (to send alerts)
  • Your postcode and chosen search radius
  • The radar type you are interested in (for now, dentists)
  • Basic usage data, such as pages visited and searches performed

3. How we use your data

We use this information to:

  • Send you alerts when matching availability is detected
  • Improve the service, for example by understanding demand by area
  • Monitor performance, reliability, and security of the service

4. Email and communication

Your email address is used to deliver alerts and occasional important updates about the service. You can stop receiving alerts at any time by using the link provided in emails where available, or by contacting us directly.

5. Cookies and analytics

We may use basic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand aggregated usage patterns. This helps us improve the reliability and usability of DentistRadar. These tools may set cookies in your browser.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be stored with reputable third-party providers (such as hosting or email services) solely for the purpose of operating the service.

7. Data retention

We retain your information for as long as is necessary to provide alerts and to run the service. You can request removal of your data by contacting us.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights relating to your personal data, such as the right to access, correct, or delete your data.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is used, please contact: support@dentistradar.co.uk.
