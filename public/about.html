About

Dentist Radar helps NHS patients find availability faster by sending email alerts when practices near you appear to accept new NHS patients. We use publicly available NHS pages and keep your data minimal and private.

Why we built this

Finding an NHS dentist can be slow and frustrating. We created Dentist Radar to save you time — and to give you a fair chance to secure a place when openings appear.

How it works

  • You add your email, postcode, and radius.
  • We check public NHS sources on a schedule.
  • We email you when a nearby practice appears to accept new NHS patients.

Note: Always double-check with the practice before you travel.
