About Dentist Radar

Finding an NHS dentist shouldn’t feel impossible. We monitor official NHS listings and email you when a nearby practice starts accepting new patients.

We built Dentist Radar after experiencing the same frustration ourselves. The goal is simple: save you time and stress by checking NHS pages regularly and notifying you — so you don’t have to keep refreshing websites.

  • Verified NHS data

    We use publicly available NHS pages as our source.

  • Automatic checks

    We check on a schedule and email you only when there’s a change.

  • Privacy first

    GDPR aware. No spam. Unsubscribe any time.
