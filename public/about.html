About DentistRadar

Helping people find NHS dentists faster, not months later.

DentistRadar was created after hearing the same story again and again: people calling dozens of practices, checking NHS pages daily, and still not finding an NHS dentist. So instead of refreshing pages, we let the system do the watching – and send you an alert the moment a practice opens.

What DentistRadar does

DentistRadar automatically checks NHS England data for practices accepting new NHS patients within your chosen radius. When a match appears, you get an email alert so you can contact the practice before spaces fill up.

Our principles are simple:

  • Use official NHS England data wherever possible
  • Scan regularly so you don’t have to
  • Keep the experience simple and easy to understand
  • Respect your inbox and your privacy
Part of the HealthRadar platform

More than just dentists

DentistRadar is the first part of a wider HealthRadar platform, aiming to make it easier to access NHS services – from dentists to GPs and beyond.

We’re starting with NHS dentists in England, then expanding to other parts of the UK and other types of care.

If you have feedback, stories, or suggestions, please reach out: support@dentistradar.co.uk.
