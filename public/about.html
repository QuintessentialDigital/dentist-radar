About Dentist Radar

We help people find NHS dental availability quickly and simply.

Our Mission

Dentist Radar turns NHS availability data into timely, easy-to-use alerts, so you can act fast when practices near you start accepting new NHS patients. We focus on clarity, privacy, and speed.

How It Works

Data We monitor official NHS listings for changes in patient acceptance status.
Scanning Our system checks at regular intervals and flags changes near your postcode.
Alerts When there’s availability, we email you so you can contact the practice promptly.
Privacy We store the minimum necessary data (email, postcode, radius) to deliver alerts.
