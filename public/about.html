About Dentist Radar

Dentist Radar is a simple, automated alert service that helps people find NHS dental practices accepting new patients.

We monitor publicly available NHS practice information and notify subscribers by email as soon as a nearby dentist starts taking new patients. Our goal is to save you the time and frustration of manually checking every local practice.
