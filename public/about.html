About DentistRadar

We help people find NHS dental availability quickly and simply.

Our Mission

DentistRadar turns NHS availability data into timely, easy-to-use alerts, so you can act fast when practices near you start accepting new NHS patients. We focus on clarity, privacy, and speed.

How It Works

DataWe monitor official NHS listings for changes in patient acceptance status.
ScanningOur system checks every hour and flags changes near your postcode.
AlertsWhen there’s availability, we email you instantly so you can contact the practice promptly.
PrivacyWe store only the minimum data needed (email, postcode, radius) and never sell it.
