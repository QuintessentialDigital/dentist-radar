Frequently asked questions

Who can become a Partner Clinic?

DentistRadar Partner Clinics are UK-registered dental practices that provide NHS and/or private care. We currently focus on practices in England and will extend to other nations over time.

How do you show Partner Clinics to patients?

When people use DentistRadar to look for a dentist in their area, we first check whether any local practices are clearly accepting patients. If none are available, we highlight Partner Clinics who can offer private or mixed NHS/private treatment nearby.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. Plans are billed monthly and can be cancelled at any time before the next billing date.

Do you guarantee a certain number of patients?

We do not guarantee specific numbers, because demand varies by area. However, we focus on people who are already searching in your postcode, so the interest level is very high. We will share simple usage metrics with you over time.

How do we get started?

Submit your details using the form above. We’ll review your practice, confirm the best plan for your area, and send you a secure payment link when you’re ready to go ahead.
